# COS Launch Hub

This is the master repository for **Continental Operation Services (COS)** -- a multi-brand digital and business ecosystem including:

- COS Motors (Vehicles & Automotive)
- COS Promotions (Events & Marketing)
- COS Music Group (Production & Distribution)
- COS Underdogs (Startups & Talent)
- Sin City Movement (Las Vegas Projects)
- Team COS (Personal Brand & Social Media)
